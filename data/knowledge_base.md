# City emergency operations — knowledge base (synthetic)

## Utilities — water main break
- Shut affected valves when safe; coordinate with traffic for excavation.
- Notify hospitals and care facilities in the outage polygon.
- Public message: boil-water advisory until tests clear.

## Flood response
- Monitor river gauges; pre-stage pumps in sectors 6–9.
- Evacuation routes: Highway 12 northbound, Metro Line B (special service).

## Cyber incident
- Isolate affected VLANs; preserve logs for forensic team.
- Do not pay ransom; activate city cyber incident response plan (CIRP).

## Public safety
- Use approved templates only for active-threat communications.
- Primary liaison: city EOC safety desk.
